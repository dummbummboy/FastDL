Alsace - Bomb/Defuse
Beta 1
by Fregira

Terrorists are threatening the peace
of a small Alsation town.

Counter-Terrorists: Prevent Terrorists
from bombing the wine deliveries.
Team members must defuse any bombs 
that threaten targeted areas.

Terrorists: The Terrorist carrying the
C4 must destroy one of the wine 
delivery  stashes. 

Other Notes: There are 2 targets in the mission.
