Trailerpark - Hostage Rescue
by Cmdr

Original Mapdesign by Chris Auty

Textures by Cmdr, Running with Scissors, textures.com
Music by Michael Ramir C
Sounds by freesound.org

This map is a complete recreation of the cutted
official CS:CZ map cs_trailerpark made by Chris Auty.
The creation of this remake is based on leaked
screenshots of the original map.

Counter-Terrorists: Enter the old 
trailerpark to rescue the hostages.
Take out the Terrorists without jeopardizing 
the hostages.

Terrorists: Prevent Counter-Terrorist
force from rescuing the hostages.

Other Notes: There are 4 hostages in 
the mission.
